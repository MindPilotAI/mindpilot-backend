How to read this Cognitive Flight Report
MindPilot is your critical thinking copilot. This Cognitive Flight Report – sometimes also described as a Reasoning Diagnostic Report or Logic Integrity Report – highlights patterns in reasoning: logical fallacies, cognitive biases, rhetorical framing, and manipulative patterns.

Important: MindPilot does not act as a fact checker and does not independently verify whether specific claims or statements are true. It focuses on how arguments are made, not on adjudicating real-world accuracy.

Use this report as a lens – a kind of Reasoning X-Ray – to reflect on structure, fairness, and persuasion in what you're hearing or reading. It is designed to be explainable and auditable, not a black-box verdict.
